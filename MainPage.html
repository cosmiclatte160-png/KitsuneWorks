Our Games

Check out our games here!

Senja

Senja is a 3D survival horror game where you play as Hafiz, a boy completing his evening chores while following Islamic practices. Because of an antique coin, unseen entities begin to haunt the house. Every missed prayer or neglected du'a increases the danger. Manage your time and stay disciplined.

Hoshimori Cafe

At Hoshimori Cafe, every cup tells a story. Tokiya Souta, a new barista, quickly learns that his job isn't just about serving coffee but it's also about understanding people. Customers come in with different personalities, careers and hidden worries. By listening carefully, Souta must secretly craft the perfect coffee that matches who they are. Souta realizes that choosing the wrong drink will push the customers away while choosing the right drink not only comforts the customer but also unlocks deeper stories about their lives.

Through this journey, players discover that coffee isn't "just a drink", but a reflection of personality, mood and connection which is a bridge between people.

Out of the Woods

Out of the Woods is a survival game set in a dark forest where you play as a lost hiker trying to make it through the night. Explore your surroundings, avoid dangers like a lurking bear, and make smart decisions to stay alive. With limited visibility and resources, every move counts in this tense and immersive survival experience.

LunaR

LunaR is an engaging 2D endless runner game that challenges players to react quickly and stay focused. Players must avoid obstacles, collect rewards, and keep running to achieve the highest score possible. As the game progresses, the speed and difficulty increase, making it more exciting and competitive for players of all skill levels.

Yume Mindscape

Yume Mindscape is a 2D pixel game about a high school girl preparing for her math exams. Players help her answer quiz questions to move forward in the game.
